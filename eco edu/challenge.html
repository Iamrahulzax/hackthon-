Challenge Overview

Go one week without producing any waste. Track everything you consume and find creative ways to reuse or avoid single-use items. This challenge will help you develop mindful consumption habits and reduce your environmental impact.

Objectives:
  • Track all waste produced for 7 consecutive days
  • Use reusable alternatives for single-use items
  • Document your progress and share tips with others
  • Reflect on your consumption patterns
